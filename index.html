About Me

Discover My Journey

From healthcare operations to data-driven decision making

With over 7 years of hands-on experience in healthcare operations, I bring a unique perspective to data analytics — one rooted in the realities of patient care, insurance claims processing, hospital administration, and revenue cycle management.

My journey began at the front desk of a busy hospital, where I witnessed firsthand how data gaps lead to inefficiencies, delayed reimbursements, and suboptimal patient outcomes. This inspired me to pursue advanced training in data analytics, machine learning, and business intelligence, equipping me with the tools to transform raw healthcare data into actionable insights.

Today, I combine clinical domain knowledge with technical proficiency in Python, R, SQL, Power BI, and machine learning frameworks to build predictive models, design interactive dashboards, and optimize healthcare operations for measurable impact.
